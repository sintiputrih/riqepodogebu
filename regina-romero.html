Regina Romero Wiki (Mayor) Age, Husband, Bio, Family &amp; More

Regina Romero (born in 1979) is an American Democrat, Mayor, and Politician from Somerton, Arizona. On November 05, 2019, she gained huge media attention after winning the general election.

She became the first Latina who will serve as a mayor of Tucson, Arizona in December 2019. In fact, Regina is the only Latina mayor in the 50 largest cities in the USA. Earlier, she worked as a city council member for many years.

Furthermore, Romero defeated her opponents Ed Ackerley and Mike Cease (Green Party candidate) by 87,000 ballots. Next month, she will succeed Jonathan Rothschild (current Mayor).

Political Career, Elections & Net worth

The 40-years-old politician began her career in 2005 and served as a council aide. She became the first lady to represent Tucson’s Ward 1 as a city council member in 2007. Her responsibilities were such as provides equal pay as well as providing sick and parental leave for the City of Tucson families.

According to a source, the politician fought and won the attention of a Cesar Chavez holiday for Tucson. In 2010, Tucson’s then-mayor approved her economic recovery plan to repair the economic development strategy.

Moreover, Romero is also the co-founder of the César Chavez Holiday Coalition and Las Adelitas. Las Adelitas is the first advocacy company in Arizona committed to establishing Latina political power.

The new mayor of Tucson is even one of the founders and members of the board of directors of the Arizona Association of Latino Elected Officials (AALEO). Regina Romero’s accumulated net worth is $500,000 US Dollars, as of 2019.
