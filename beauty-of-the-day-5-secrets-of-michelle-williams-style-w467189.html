Beauty of the Day: 5 Secrets of Michelle Williams' Style

Credit:

She Ties One On

Known as a “fashion girl” on the red carpet, Williams, who’s co-starred in Kenneth Lonergan’s acclaimed drama Manchester by the Sea (which made us cry buckets) always brings her A-game. And that's won her one of the most coveted designer contracts around — hello, Louis Vuitton! For the 2017 Golden Globes, stylist Kate Young added a cool accessory, a simple black velvet ribbon as a choker, to toughen up a white lace overlaid Louis Vuitton gown. Her glam platinum pixie was trimmed by mane man Chris McMillan just before hitting the carpet with gal pal Busy Phillipps.
