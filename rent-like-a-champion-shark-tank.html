The Journey of Rent Like A Champion

Drew Mitchell and Mike Doyle embarked on an extraordinary journey when they founded Rent Like A Champion in 2006. It all began with their vacant apartment near Notre Dame football games, which they decided to list as a rental property. Their innovative idea quickly gained traction, and in 2010, they expanded their business to other college towns.

Rent Like A Champion caters specifically to college football fans, providing them with game day rentals near stadiums. Homeowners in college towns with prominent football programs play a crucial role in the company’s success. They list their homes on the platform, enabling large groups of fans to stay together during game weekends. This unique concept has transformed the game day experience for fans, fostering a sense of community and camaraderie.

Over the years, Rent Like A Champion has witnessed remarkable growth. They now offer rentals in 21 college towns across the United States, providing football enthusiasts with convenient and affordable lodging options. Their commitment to delivering an exceptional experience has propelled them to become a prominent player in the vacation rental market.

Commission and Surcharge

Rent Like A Champion charges homeowners a 15% commission on each booking. This commission covers the services provided by Rent Like A Champion, including listing promotion, property management, and customer support. Therefore, homeowners can confidently list their properties on the platform, knowing that Rent Like A Champion will handle all the necessary logistics.

Renters, on the other hand, are charged an 8% surcharge. This surcharge helps support Rent Like A Champion’s operations and allows them to continuously improve their services, ensuring an exceptional experience for both homeowners and renters alike.

Comprehensive Insurance Coverage

Rent Like A Champion understands the importance of protecting homeowners and their properties.
