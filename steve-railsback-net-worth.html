Steve Railsback (born 16 November 1945) is an American theatre, film and television actor, born in Dallas, Texas. He is best known for his performances in the films The Stunt Man and his portrayal of Charles Manson in the television miniseries Helter Skelter. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
