G-Eazy: Career

Gerald started music as an early teen and started working as a record producer.

He was in a local hip-hop group and used to release their music by MySpace. Earlier, he used to produce songs for another artist before he started rapping.

In 2009, G-Eazy released his debut studio album that had his mixtapes that caught listeners’ attention.

It got him to open shows for artists like Snoop Dog and Lil Wayne. He started his “These Things Happen” tour in 2011.

He released his debut album through a big label in 2014 and debuted at number 1 on US Rap Chart and number 3 on Billboard 200 Chart.

His other album, “When It’s Dark Out,” was released in 2015 and was on number 1 on US Rap and US R and B Charts.

The album was number 5 on Billboard 200 chart. His track “Me, Myself and I” was a big hit number 7 on the chart.

By 2016, he went on his second world tour, which sold a million tickets worldwide.

He started collaborating with big musicians like Britney Spears, Chris Brown, Kehlani, and Halsey.

In 2017, his new studio album “The Beautiful and Damned” was released that got him mixed critical reviews.

Till now, he has released multiple singles and collaborated songs that have hit the charts multiple times.

He has won a People’s Choice Award for Favorite Hip-Hop Artist.

He has achieved 13 New RIAA Certifications with a total of 21 of gold and platinum.’
