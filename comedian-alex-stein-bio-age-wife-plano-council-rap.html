Who Is Comedian Alex Stein? His Bio, Age, Wife, Plano Council Rap

Meet Comedian Alex Stein who went viral on Twitter with his comedy rap bit concerning Russia’s invasion of Ukraine.

This article touches on the subject related to his career, age, and wife. Keep reading to learn more about Alex.

Comedian Alex Stein Went Viral With Plano Council Rap

Comedian Alex Stein went viral after rapping about Ukraine and Vladimir Putin at a Plano City Council meeting. He presented himself wearing a navy blue suit and yellow tie and began rapping about the Ukraine-Russia war. The video has earned 6.9 million views on Twitter whereas the same video has been watched 107K times on his YouTube.

Alex introduces himself as “Primetime 99 Alex Stein” and raps, “Go insane, for Ukraine, a bullet in Putin’s brain!”. He also paid tribute to Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy: “Zelenskyy! Is a VIP! Ukraine and I go insane.” “I am a Zelenskyy stan! I love you, Volodymyr Zelenskyy!” he added.

He rapped further, “One more time, I’m 99, you know I grind and shine. Gas prices way too high, Vladimir Putin, needs to die!”.

Alex explained that he intended to draw attention to what was going on in Ukraine by doing something absurd so that people would look up more information on Russia’s invasion on Google.

Alex isn’t the first person to go viral after trolling a political gathering. That is, in fact, the foundation of his entire social media career. His Instagram feed contains videos of him trolling meetings in Hawaii, Houston, Denver, and even Wellington, New Zealand. He rapped about vaccination in January 2022.

He intends to do additional segments on Ukraine, and he’s willing to do a second rap performance on the subject at a council meeting with a better microphone.

Fun Fact: Business Insider did an article on him after losing $70,000 on fantasy football. He loves watching football, playing football, and betting on football. As reported he was in five fantasy football leagues.

Comedian Alex Stein Career

Comedian Alex Stein is known for having a big personality. Alex is a gigantic shrimp, with his towering stature and imposing demeanor. This one-of-a-kind blend of flair and personality has been a breath of new air in the Los Angeles comedy scene.

Alex relocated to LA after winning a campus-wide comedy competition at LSU. He is originally from Dallas, Texas. He knew comedy was for him from the moment he ascended the stage in front of over 1,000 people to accept the title of LSU’s funniest man. 99 felt it was time to chase his goals and make things happen after graduating from LSU. Alex has quickly risen through the ranks in LA, playing at the Laugh Factory and other notable venues.

Alex has quickly risen through the ranks in Los Angeles, appearing at the Laugh Factory, Comedy Store, Improv, Flappers, and most recently as the headliner at the Los Angeles Raw Artists Showcase. He’s a popular favorite thanks to his quick wit, distinctive writing, and unique delivery style. With Alex Stein, no subject is off-limits or taboo.

He has made bits about “daughter identifies as a lizard,” “Wife’s new boyfriend,” and many more.

According to IMDB, Alex has received three acting credits for appearing on Sweet Home Alabama, The Glass House, and Excused. He was also a contestant on a reality cooking show Worst Cooks In America in 2013.

When he is not pursuing comedy, Alex works in a car dealership.
