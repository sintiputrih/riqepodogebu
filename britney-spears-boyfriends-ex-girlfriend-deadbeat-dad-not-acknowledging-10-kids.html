Britney Spears Boyfriends Ex Slams Him For Being A Deadbeat Dad And Not Acknowledging His 10 K

Highlights

  • Nicole Mancilla, ex-girlfriend of Britney Spears' boyfriend Paul Soliz, claims he neglects their kids for Britney.
  • Sandra Smith, Mancilla's mother, says Paul doesn't support any of his 10 kids, including their 1-year-old.
  • Despite recent allegations and scuffles, Britney and Paul have not commented on their future or relationship status yet.

Britney Spears has continued to see her former employee Paul Richard Soliz, despite concern over his criminal history, from both her team and fans alike. But now someone else is speaking out against him –and seeming to blame Britney, too – his ex-girlfriend, who says Paul is a dead-beat dad.

Nicole Mancilla, who shares five children with Soliz, recently spoke to Daily Mail claiming her ex doesn’t live up to his responsibilities as a dad. Not only does she say he fails to pay regular child support, but Nicole claims he’s constantly expanding is brood, and is now a father of ten.

Britney’s Boyfriend Has 10 Kids, But Doesn’t Always Pay Child Support

Mancilla says things changed when Paul developed feelings for Britney, who has a history of carrying on with men in relationships (including Kevin Federline, who was with the mother of his oldest children when his romance with Spears began).

“My husband fell in Britney Spears’ p—y,” she told the publication. “He was married, and now he denies his children. He neglects his children for her.”

Nicole says Paul stays with Britney most of the time, though he lists another address as his legal places of residence. “He’s on probation, so if he officially lives at her house, they have access to searching her house,” she explained. “So, that’s why he still has an address here.”

Sandra Smith, Mancilla’s mother, also spoke to Daily Mail, revealing Nicole and Paul’s youngest child was born only a year ago. “He has 10 kids. He doesn’t support any of them,” the surgical assistant said. “They have a 1-year-old together, and he doesn’t even pay attention to that one either.”

Nicole and her five kids live with her mother, who blames their precarious financial situation on Paul. “[He] doesn’t support one kid, and they’re at my house,” she said.

“Nicole’s torn up by everything. This has been going on for five years. And he doesn’t leave her alone; he keeps coming back,” she went on. “I wouldn’t let him see the kids if it was me. She does because she wants them to have a dad. But I wouldn’t want a dad like that.”

Britney Has Said She Wants More Kids In The Future

Neither Paul nor Britney have commented on the recent claims, but with Britney’s previous admissions about wanting more kids, and her romance with Paul seeming to become serious in a short time, it’s left fans wondering what their plans for the future may be.

However, Britney and Paul got into a scuffle last week, resulting in paramedics being called to the Chateau Marmont in Hollywood. The news broke shortly after Britney’s divorce from Sam Asghari was finalized; some reports claim Britney cheated on Sam with Paul when he was under her employment, though it hasn’t been confirmed.

Britney has since slammed paramedics for invading her privacy, claiming she merely injured her ankle in an accidental fall, denying allegations of a fight. She did, however, place the blame on her mom, Lynne Spears, for the ordeal, though Lynne didn’t seem to be present at the time of the fight.
