Video: Stacy Amoateng Confirms Management Role At Angel FM And Angel TV

Popular Ghanaian TV presenter, Stacy Amoateng has indeed taken over the management of Angel TV and Angel FM Accra, a subsidiary of the Angel Group of Companies owned by Dr Kwaku Oteng.

Her new role as the General Manager comes after reports that Dr. Oteng has allegedly sacked Sally Akua Amoakowaa Mensah aka Akua GMB.

Taking to her IG page, Stacy Amoateng shared some videos of the launch of Angel FM to confirm that indeed, she now plays a managerial role over there.
