Who is Emily Watson:

Emily Watson is a famous Movie Actress. She was born on January 14, 1967 and her birthplace is Islington, England. Emily is also well known as, English actress well-known for her performance as Bess in the award-winning indie film Breaking the Waves. In 2017 she appeared in the National Geographic original TV series Genius. In 2019, she began starring in the HBO historical miniseries, Chernobyl.

Emily is originated from England. Helena Bonham Carter turned down the role in Breaking the Waves that eventually went to Watson.
