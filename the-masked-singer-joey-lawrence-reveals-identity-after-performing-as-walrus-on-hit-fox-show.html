The Masked Singer: Joey Lawrence reveals identity after performing as Walrus on hit Fox show

Joey Lawrence revealed his identity after performing as the Walrus on Sunday’s double-elimination episode of The Masked Singer on Fox.

The 46-year-old actor wore a giant Walrus mask along with a yellow raincoat as he performed Two Princes by the Spin Doctors during ’90s Night on season eight.

‘You just lit a fire under ’90s Night,’ Nicole Scherzinger, 44, said before his unmasking.

When his identity was revealed Jenny McCarthy, 50, encouraged him to record albums.

‘I’m going to start doing some music again,’ Joey said after he took off his Walrus mask costume. ‘It will be fun.’

‘You should,’ Robin Thicke, 45, said. ‘You sounded great.’

Show host Nick Cannon, 42, asked him whether he had fun being on the show.

‘It’s a blast,’ Joey said. ‘I always got blown up on IG that you guys were guessing it was me in past seasons. So then Fox finally said they want you to come on and I said ‘Sure, let’s just do this.’

‘I just said Joey Lawrence to everyone and I finally got it right,’ Robin quipped.

Joey was the first contestant and his clues included: signing autographs, a catch phrase and a cat.

NSYNC’s Lance Bass, 43, took the stage and delivered another clue to Walrus’ identity.

‘I knew I smelled a boy bander in the building,’ said Jenny, who was dressed as Gwen Stefani.

The clue was a mix tape of TV theme songs.

Before the unmasking, Ken Jeong, 53, guessed it was John Stamos. Jenny guessed it might be the actor Scott Wolf. Nicole thought it might be Mario Lopez. Robin, though, rightly insisted that it was Joey Lawrence.

Hip Hop duo Tag Team opened the show performing their hit song Whoomp! (There It Is).

The Milkshake mask, a giant pink shake topped with strawberries, went next. His clues included peach pits, breaking records, balls, and rockets. The Milkshake performed Jump on It by Sir Mix A Lot.

Milkshake mask: The Milkshake mask, a giant pink shake topped with strawberries, went next

‘That is the most fun performance I think we have seen this season,’ Ken said.

Boy Meets World actress Dannielle Fishel, 41, delivered his additional clue. The clue was a playlist for an afterparty and mentioned Wiz and Snoop Dog.

Ken thought it might be LL Cool J. Jenny thought maybe it could be DJ Jazzy Jeff. Nicole thought maybe T.I. Robin thought it might be the football player DeSean Jackson.

Another clue: Boy Meets World actress Dannielle Fishel, 41, delivered an additional clue

All the judges were wrong. NFL Running back Le’Veon Bell, 30, was the second person voted off.

‘I did not see this coming,’ Ken said.

‘You killed it!’ Nick said.

Surprise reaction: Robin Thicke, Jenny McCarthy, Ken Jeon and Nicole Scherzinger were wrong about Milkshake

Nick asked him what made him do the Masked Singer.

‘I’m really trying to showcase my talents,’ Le’veon said. ‘ A lot of people know me for the football field. I just wanted to get in another lane and kind of show people that I’m more talented in other things than just football.’

‘You did it here tonight,’ Nick said.

The three little Lambs, the reigning queens of the show shared new clues to their identity of popcorn and a movie. They took the stage together and performed Ironic by Alanis Morissette.

‘Your voices are incredible,’ Nicole said. ‘You sound like a record up there.’

Lance returned to give out a clue.

‘You can now call me lambs Bass,’ Lance said.

The clue included reality and day trips.

Nicole said she thought it might be The Corrs singing group. Robin said it was The Chicks.

The Lambs ultimately beat out Milkshake in the Battle Royale with each singing their version of Haddaway’s song What Is Love.

The Lambs moved on to the semi finals.

The Masked Singer will return next Wednesday on Fox.
