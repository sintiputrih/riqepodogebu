Introducing the Twin Daughters of Love and Hip Hop Miami Star, Amara La Negra


Amara La Negra, the star of Love and Hip Hop Miami, has joyfully introduced her twin daughters to the world.

She expressed in her caption: “I Present to the World My Daughters  #SumajestadRoyalty    #SualtezaEmpress”

Amara La Negra welcomed the twin girls with her partner, businessman Allan Muses.
