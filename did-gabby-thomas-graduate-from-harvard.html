Did Gabby Thomas graduate from Harvard?

Gabby Thomas is an accomplished athlete who has made a significant impact on the track, but she is also noteworthy for her academic achievements.

Gabby Thomas' Educational Journey at Harvard University

Gabby Thomas attended Harvard University, where she not only excelled on the track but also demonstrated remarkable academic prowess.

She earned an undergraduate degree in Neurobiology and Global Health & Health Policy at Harvard University and is currently pursuing a Master's degree in Public Health.

Track and Field Achievements

Gabby Thomas has made a name for herself as a formidable force in the world of track and field. Her athletic accomplishments are truly remarkable, and they have solidified her position as one of the most talented athletes in the sport. Some of her notable accolades in track and field include:

In addition to her outstanding athletic achievements, Gabby Thomas has also demonstrated an unwavering commitment to academic excellence.

Transition to Professional Athletics

After her illustrious collegiate career, Gabby Thomas signed a contract with New Balance and turned professional in October 2018, forgoing her last year of collegiate eligibility.

This transition marked a pivotal moment in her athletic career, and she has continued to shine as a professional athlete, further solidifying her status as a prominent figure in the world of track and field.
