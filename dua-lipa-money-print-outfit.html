Dua Lipa Looks Rich in a Sheer Money-Print Outfit

Dua Lipa's latest night out look is all about the Benjamins.

The "Sweetest Pie" singer was spotted out in her native London last night, adding another mark to the list of reasons why she's become a bonafide street-style star. The pop star wore a nod to the wealth she's been raking in on her recent European tour, donning a long-sleeved crop top and biker short set, which were semi-sheer and decorated with the print and lettering from an American hundred-dollar bill.

The "Don't Stop Now" singer wore the set with black lingerie that showed through the print, and matching green lace-up stiletto boots. She completed the look with silver jewelry including a butterfly chain belt, a necklace with a large cowboy boot pendant, a sparkly silver shoulder bag, and an assortment of ring. She also matched her glittery eyeshadow to the green and silver theme.

Ricky Vigil M//Getty Images

Lipa also added the outfit to her sartorial showcase on Instagram, captioning a carousel of pics taken in the backseat of a car with the caption, "backseat freestyle."

Yesterday's money look was the Grammy winner's second time turning to the sheer trend in so many weeks. Last weekend, Lipa shared a similar all-black outfit alongside several behind-the-scenes pics from her tour. For the monochromatic look, she layered a black bra and a pair of boy shorts under a mesh top and leggings. Her accessories included a rhinestone choker with a statement cherry pendant and a yellow-and-black plaid shoulder bag.
