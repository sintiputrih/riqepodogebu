Russell Wilson involved in bizarre golf cart accident as fans joke 'just another drive ending in a t

DENVER Broncos star Russell Wilson has reportedly been involved in a bizarre golf cart accident.

And football fans couldn't stop themselves from poking fun at the nine-time Pro Bowl quarterback, 34, particularly as the incident took place at a golf course that shares the name with an NFL arena.

Wilson's cart reportedly flipped into a bunker as the NFL star played at Arrowhead Golf Course in Littleton, Colorado on Saturday, according to 104.3 The Fan's host Mat Smith.

No one got injured in the accident, per reports, but it is also unclear what caused the cart to flip over.

But as the news about the crash spread, NFL fans joked Wilson's misfortunes from his first season as a Bronco seem to continue.

Others brought up the quarterback's poor record at Arrowhead, the home of the Kansas City Chiefs.

One tweeted: "Just another drive ending in a turnover."

Another wrote: "To be fair, this isn’t the first time Russ has struggled at Arrowhead."

A third said: "If anything perfectly sums up his Broncos career….."

And one added: "Just another sack, no big deal."

Wilson had a dreadful season after moving from the Seattle Seahawks to the Broncos in 2022.

He registered career lows in touchdown percentage (3.3percent), completion percentage (60.5percent), and net yards gained per pass attempt (5.87) last year.

The Ohio native also logged a net rating below 90 on the season (84.4) for the first time since entering the NFL in 2012.

In the penultimate week of the 2022 campaign, Wilson played at Arrowhead for the second time ever — and was defeated again with the Broncos taking a 27-24 loss.

In those two games, the QB got twice as many sacks (six) as passing touchdowns (three) in addition to throwing one interception.

Barstool Sports later reported Wilson was in the cart but didn't drive it during the accident.
