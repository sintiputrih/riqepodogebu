Career Highlights and Achievements

  • Josh Groban’s musical journey has been nothing short of remarkable. He has released a series of successful studio albums, including “Josh Groban,” “Closer,” “Awake,” “All That Echoes,” “Stages,” “Bridges,” and “Harmony.”
  • Not only a singer, but Josh has also showcased his acting talent in various movies like “Coffee Town,” “Muppets Most Wanted,” “The Hollars,” and “Crazy, Stupid, Love.” Additionally, he has graced several television series, including “Ally McBeal,” “American Dad!,” “Never Mind the Buzzcocks,” “Glee,” “Robot Chicken,” “Live With Regis and Kelly,” “Repeat After Me,” and “That’s My Jam.”
