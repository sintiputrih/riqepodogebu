Doug McClure is one of the most popular and richest TV Actor who was born on May 11, 1935 in Glendale, California, United States. Actor who starred as the cowboy Trampas on the NBC TV series The Virginian, and in lead roles on Checkmate, Overland Trail, and Out of This World. And he was the inspiration for The Simpsons character Troy McClure.

He married five times and had two children, daughters Valerie and Tane, the latter of whom also became an actor.
