Debby Ryan Pics Of The Disney Channel Star Hollywood Life

Debby Ryan in the front row Elie Saab’s  Spring/Summer 2020 show at Paris Fashion Week in France on September 28, 2019. 

Debby Ryan, born on May 13, 1993 in Huntsville, Alabama, is an American actress and singer After she started acting in theater, she was discovered by Disney Channel, which led to her becoming one of the popular faces of the network in the early 2000s. Her first main role was as Bailey Pickett on The Suite Life on Deck from 2008 to 201. She later appeared in the 2010 Disney Channel Original Movie, 16 Wishes. In 2011, Debby landed her own Disney Channel series, Jessie, in which she starred as the titular character. After moving on from Disney, Debby starred as Patty Bladell in the Netflix series Insatiable. During her time on the Disney Channel, Debby recorded music for the shows she was on. In 2013, she formed the band, The Never Ending and toured with them as an opening act for the North American leg of Fifth Harmony’s Reflection Tour in 2015. Debby currently lives in California. She got engaged to Twenty One Pilots drummer, Joshua Dun in December 2018. They tied the knot in secret on New Year’s Eve in 2019/20 in Austin, Texas.
