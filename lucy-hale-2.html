Career

Lucy Hale started her career as a singer in the year 2003. She has participated in the popular TV reality show American Juniors and finished with fourth place in the show. After that, she also formed a group with other finalists and released several albums and singles. Later in the year 2005, the group disbanded due to some unknown reasons after their album titled Lackluster Sales.

After that, she moved to Los Angeles in search of some musical work but later she also started giving auditions for minor roles in Tv series and films. Luckily, she received a minor role in the TV series titled Drake & Josh. She also played small cameos in TV series like Ned’s Declassified School Survival Guide, The O.C., and How I Met Your Mother.

Since then she has made her appearance in many popular series Wizards of Waverly Place, Bionic Woman, Life Sentence, and several others. After that in the year 2008, she made her big-screen debut with her appearance in the popular film titled The Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants 2.

She has worked in many other popular films like Privileged, Sorority Wars, Scream 4, Secret of Wings, Truth or Dare, Dude, The Unicorn, Fantasy Island, The Hating Game, and many other popular movies. She also received an offer of singing in the popular series A Cinderella Story Series and sang One upon a song.

She also made her appearance in popular music videos like She Burns, Lose Control, Comes Back Down to Earth, and others. She also worked as a backup singer with popular singer Carly Pearce.

In the year 2012, she signed a deal with Hollywood Records and released her first album titled Road Between in the year 2014. After that, she also recorded songs like Mistletoe and You’re Here in the year 2017.

Awards

Lucy Hale has won many prestigious awards and nominations throughout her career in the music and acting industry. She has won the Teen Choice Awards seven times in her career in different categories for her incredible work in the series Pretty Little Liars.

She has also won the Young Hollywood Awards twice in her career in various categories in the year 2011 and 2013. She has also received the Gracie Allen Awards and People’s Choice Awards in the years 2013 and 2014.
