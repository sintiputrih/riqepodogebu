Who Is Amy Poehlers Ex-Husband Will Arnett?

Will Arnett, the ex-husband of Amy Poehler, is a Canadian actor and comedian who is known to many as the ex-lover of Amy. Will Arnett and Amy Poehler walked down the aisle in 2003 and they officially divorced in 2016.

Will Arnett is known in real life as William Emerson Arnett and he was born on May 4, 1970, hence, he is 51 years of age. Will Arnett has been active in the media scenes for almost three decades.

Will Arnett was born to Edith Alexandra Palk and Emerson James Arnett, and he is an alumnus of Concordia University and Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute.

Will Arnett bagged the Outstanding Achievement for Voice Acting in an Animated Television/Broadcast Production and the Annie Awards and also bagged the Best Voice Actor in an Animated Series at the Critics’ Choice Super Awards.

Some of the movies starred by Will Arnett include Monsters vs Aliens, The Nut Job, Let’s Go to Prison, Blades of Glory, Ratatouille, The LEGO Batman Movie, among others.
