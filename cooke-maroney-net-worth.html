Cooke Maroney Career Success

Before Cooke Maroney became well-known for being associated with actress Jennifer Lawrence, he used to attend fancy art events in New York. As early as October 28, 2011, he was seen at a laid-back end-of-summer party hosted by Fabiola Beracasa from The Hole Gallery.
