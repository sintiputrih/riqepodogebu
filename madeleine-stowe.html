Biography

Madeleine Stowe was born in Los Angeles, CA on August 18, 1958. Golden Globe-nominated actress best known for her portrayal of the villainous Victoria Grayson in the ABC series Revenge. Her film credits include 12 Monkeys, The Last of the Mohicans, and Short Cuts, the latter of which earned her the National Society of Film Critics Award for Best Supporting Actress. She starred in the 1994 film Bad Girls with Drew Barrymore. She first appeared on TV in a 1978 episode of the detective series Baretta. Her first film role was in the 1981 crime drama Gangster Wars.
