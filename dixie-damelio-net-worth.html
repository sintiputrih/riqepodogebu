What is Dixie D’Amelio’s Net Worth?

Dixie D’Amelio, also known as Dixie, is a social media influencer and singer who gained popularity through her videos on TikTok. She has a net worth of $10 million and is the older sister of fellow TikTok star Charli D’Amelio.

In 2020, Dixie signed with HitCo Entertainment and released several singles as a recording artist. She has a massive following on social media with over 35 million fans on TikTok and 25 million followers on Instagram.

Dixie D’Amelio Earns Millions from Sponsorships and Merchandise

Dixie D’Amelio, one of the most popular personalities on TikTok, earned approximately $3 million in 2020 from sponsorships, production opportunities, and merchandise deals. This made her the third-highest earner on TikTok for the year, behind her sister Charli and Addison Rae.

In 2021, Dixie’s earnings skyrocketed to $10 million from her various endeavors both on and off TikTok.

Dixie D’Amelio’s Rise to Fame

Dixie D’Amelio was born in 2001 in Norwalk, Connecticut to Heidi and Marc. Her career began in 2019 after her younger sister, Charli, became popular on TikTok. Dixie joined the platform and signed with United Talent Agency alongside her sister. She also joined The Hype House, a collective of teenage TikTok personalities based in Los Angeles. The D’Amelio sisters later signed a podcast deal with Ramble Podcast Network. By August 2020, Dixie had become the third highest-earning TikTok user, just behind her sister.

Other Social Media and Digital Platforms

Dixie D’Amelio has appeared on various social media and digital platforms. In 2020, she starred in the web series “Attaway General” on YouTube’s Brat TV network. She also hosts a talk show on YouTube, “The Early Late Night Show,” and appears with her family in “Dinner with the D’Amelios.”

In 2021, Dixie starred with her sister in the Snap Originals reality series “Charli vs. Dixie,” and with her family in the Hulu docuseries “The D’Amelio Show.”

Dixie D’Amelio’s Music Career

Dixie released her debut single “Be Happy” in June 2020, which peaked at number 55 on the UK Singles Chart and garnered over 86 million streams on Spotify in its first two months. She later signed with HitCo Entertainment and released “One Whole Day” featuring Wiz Khalifa. Dixie also collaborated with Liam Payne on “Naughty List” and released other singles such as “Roommates,” “Fuckboy,” and “Psycho” featuring Rubi Rose.

Sponsorships and Collaborations

Dixie D’Amelio has done several sponsorships and corporate collaborations alongside her sister Charli. In March of 2020, the two partnered with UNICEF for an anti-bullying campaign, and in May, they appeared in the television special “Graduate Together: America Honors the High School Class of 2020.”

In July of 2020, the D’Amelio sisters became the face of Hollister’s Jean Lab campaign, and created a TikTok dance to promote it. They also partnered with the company Morphe Cosmetics to launch their own makeup line, called Morphe 2, and with Orosa Beauty to launch a nail polish collection called Coastal Craze. In 2021, they co-created Hollister’s Social Tourist apparel brand, and became the face of its campaign. The D’Amelios also partnered with Simmons Bedding Company to design the Charlie & Dixie x Simmons Mattress.

Dixie D’Amelio’s Relationship History

Dixie D’Amelio, who resides in Los Angeles, California, has been in the public eye due to her relationships. In October of 2020, she announced that she was in a relationship with social media personality Noah Beck. Prior to this, she dated Griffin Johnson.
