Katie Holmes Daughter Suri Reportedly Dropped Dad Tom Cruises Name

Suri Cruise seems to be following in the footsteps of Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt’s kids, as she reportedly dropped “Cruise” from her name during a milestone moment. Katie Holmes and Tom Cruise’s 18-year-old daughter appears to be distancing herself from her famous dad with this latest move!

The teen graduated from La Guardia High School on Friday, where she was beaming in a red gown with her long brown hair hanging in loose waves down her back in photos obtained by Page Six. There are photos of her hugging a friend and smiling with her mom, clearly enjoying this big event. In some pictures, Suri holds her cap in one hand as she walks in heels with her gown open to reveal her white dress underneath. The Dawson’s Creek alum wore light yellow pleated pants and a matching collared shirt for the occasion.  During the ceremony, Suri reportedly went by the name “Suri Noelle” instead of “Suri Cruise,” showing that she has, at least publicly, dropped her dad’s last name. Last month, she reportedly also used “Suri Noelle” when she acted in a local theater company’s performance. Whether or not she will legally drop his name like 18-year-old Shiloh Jolie remains to be seen.
