Here's What Dua Lipa's Net Worth Actually Is

Dua Lipa's continuous hard work has clearly paid off as she is already one wealthy woman. As of 2020, the "Levitating" hitmaker is worth an astonishing $16 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. It's safe to say we should all definitely be taking note of those "rules" she once set.

In just over five years since her debut on the music scene, it's clear that Lipa has made a big impact on the entertainment industry with her infectious pop songs. According to Billboard, she has achieved two No. 1 singles and a No. 1 album in the UK. On the US Billboard Hot 100, three of her singles have peaked within the top 10. As reported by the BBC in December 2018, Lipa became the UK's most-streamed female act. That same year, Lipa made Forbes' 30 Under 30 Entertainment list at the age of 22. Since the release of 2020's Future Nostalgia, she went on to break three global records on Spotify during its first week of release, per Official Charts. 

Aside from music, Lipa has also earned money by fronting ad campaigns. In 2018, she was the face of Adidas' Statement collection, according to Hypebae. In 2019, she became the brand ambassador for Yves Saint Laurent's Libre fragrance, per Moodie Davitt Report.

As long as Lipa keeps releasing hit records and continues to grow her massive fan base, her net worth will rapidly increase in no time.
