Alexandra Fuentes Biography

Alexandra Fuentes is a Puerto Rican actress, comedian, TV producer, and radio host who was born on January 23, 1978, in Caguas, Puerto Rico as Alexandra Fuentes Rivera. She was the main host of Univision Puerto Rico talk and variety show Anda Pa’l Cara from the year 2005 to 2009, as well as the host of Telemundo Puerto Rico entertainment show Dando Candela until 2013.

Alexandra was also hosting of Fidelity 95.7 FM morning show Gozando en la Mañana, with Raymond Arrieta, Alexandra had her own talk show Alexandra De Noche “now Telemundo De Noche” on Telemundo Puerto Rico until she left the show in the year 2016 to support her husband, David Bernier, in his campaign for Governor of Puerto Rico.
