WWE Live Event Results From Paris, France (4/30): Roman Reigns, Charlotte Flair And More Compete

WWE‘s United Kingdom tour continued on April 30 when the company held a live event on April 30 at the Accor Arena in Paris, France.

Roman Reigns, Charlotte Flair, RK-Bro and other stars competed.

The results, courtesy of Wrestling Bodyslam, are available below:

  • Ricochet (c) def. Sami Zayn and Butch
  • Aliyah def. Shotzi
  • Gunther def. Shinsuke Nakamura
  • Bobby Lashley def. Ridge Holland
  •  Charlotte Flair (c) def. Ronda Rousey
  •  Sasha Banks & Naomi (c) def. Natalya & Shayna Baszler
  • RK-Bro (Randy Orton & Riddle) def. The Usos (Jimmy & Jey Uso)
  •  Roman Reigns (c) def. Drew McIntyre
