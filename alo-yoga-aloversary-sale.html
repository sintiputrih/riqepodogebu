Rosie Huntington-Whiteley's Workout Set Is From Alo Yoga

When adding new fashion pieces to your closet, particularly activewear like leggings and sports bras, you want to feel confident that they’re going to last through numerous wears and all types of repeated movements (and preferably for years). And if celebrities are fans of the brand, our interest is all the more piqued.

Alo Yoga’s activewear has been a staple in Hollywood wardrobes, worn on numerous occasions by celebrities like Katie Holmes, Jennifer Garner, and Sydney Sweeney. Kendall and Kylie Jenner (who are both brand ambassadors) also wear its pieces on repeat, along with supermodels like Alessandra Ambrosio and most recently, Rosie Huntington-Whiteley, who posed in a sponsored social post wearing a white monochromatic set.  

Huntington-Whiteley, also an Alo ambassador, embraces a neutral Instagram feed, and her outfits are certainly on par. In an Instagram carousel posted earlier this month, the model wore the Alo Yoga Airbrush High-Waist Biker Shorts, and the Splendor Bra layered under the Dreamscape Button-Down for a monochromatic white ensemble. The supermodel actress added her own flair with gold-tone jewelry, Alo’s Throwback Socks, an all-white trucker hat, and the Infamous Sunglasses that we’d undoubtedly file under the ‘clean girl aesthetic’ style. 

Alo Yoga 5-Inch Airbrush High-Waist Biker Shorts

Alo’s Airbrush line “feels like butter,” per one reviewer, and is among its most popular fabrics featured in leggings, bras, and bike shorts like the 5-inch style worn by Huntington-Whiteley. According to another shopper, the four-way stretch fabric sculpts and smoothes and “is not see-through at all,” even in white. A third person said they’re the only bike shorts that “don’t roll or ride up.” 

Alo Yoga Splendor Bra

For a matching set, you can grab Huntington-Whiteley’s Splendor Bra which offers light to medium support. It’s a solid choice for casual wear and is “very comfy, flattering, and lightweight,” as described by one shopper. Made from the same sculpting airbrush fabric as the shorts, it’s stretchy yet comfortably compressive to provide support, and it has adjustable straps to customize the fit further. One person described the style as “adorable and versatile” both in and out of the gym, adding that they felt “secure” while wearing it during a spin class. 

Head over to Alo Yoga to shop the matching bike shorts and bra, and keep scrolling for the other pieces featured in Rosie Huntington-Whiteley’s look.
