Getting into the Hollywood industry is quite tricky. It’s even harder if you are outsiders from another country. Not everybody is Arnold Schwarzenegger, right? Well, Stellan Skarsgard and Arnold might be physically different, but they both have a common attribute. They both come from another nation to become one of the most loved actors in Hollywood.

So, people say his family is filled with stars; how much of that is true? Here’s what you need to know about Stellan’s married life and children.

Who is Stellan Skarsgard? Wiki, Bio

Stellan John Skarsgard is one of the legendary actors born in Gothenburg, Sweden, to Swedish parents Gudurn and Jan on 13th June 1951.

He lived his childhood in different places; Helsingborg, Totebo, Kalmar, Uppsala, and Maryland. We don’t know at what age he moved to America, and what are his educational achievements; we know that the Swedish actor was already on the big screens at the age of 21.

Before his film debut, he had played a lead role in Swedish television ‘Bombi Bitt och jag’ in 1968. He is widely known for his role in ‘Good Evening, Mr. Wallenberg’, where he played the character of a Swedish Diplomat ‘Raoul.’

He is also known for his work as the guilt-ridden policeman Jonas Engstrom in the 1997 Norwegian film ‘Insomnia.’

He did a Hollywood movie in 1985, called ‘Noon Wine,’ which marked his debut to the Hollywood cinema world.

Further, Skarsgard is world-known for his roles in ‘Pirates of the Carribean: Dead Man’s Chest, Pirates of the Carribean: At World’s End, and Mamma Mia! here We Go Again.’

Moreover, Stellan starred as Dr. Erik Selving in Marvel’s 2011 movie ‘Thor’ and then reprised the role in 2013 sequel ‘Thor: The Dark World’ then he was seen in ‘The Avengers’ and ‘Avengers: Age of Ultron.’

Stellan Skarsgard Married Life With Megan Everett & Past Affairs

Stellan and Megan Everett are married since 2009, but before that, he was My Skarsgard’s husband of 32 years. He was married to his ex-wife, My, a physician, in April 1975. Despite being married for over three decades, the couple divorced in May 2007.

Later, he started dating Megan Everett and got hitched in January 2009. Since then, the duo is living happily with their children.
