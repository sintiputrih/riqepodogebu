MTV released a teaser trailer for Siesta Key Season 5 on 22nd February 2022. The preview gave viewers a glimpse of what is to come in the new season. The rumored girlfriend is Jordana Barnes and fans should remember her from the previous season as well.

Kim Marie Leto, a 50-year-old, had struck Brenden and likely his friend, who were in the westbound bike lane along Whitfield Avenue near Tuttle Avenue in Florida, at about 10:30 p.m. on a Saturday night.
