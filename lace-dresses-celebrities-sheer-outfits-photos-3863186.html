32 Stars Slaying In Sexy Lace Dresses For Any Season: Halsey, Kendall Jenner &amp; More

Lace dresses are one hot trend, and they're perfect for any time of year. Stars like Halsey, Kendall Jenner, Kylie Jenner and more have slayed lacy style over the years.

If you still want to get dressed up for your at-home holiday celebration this year, lace dresses are the way to go. The look can be extremely versatile, and is always super sexy. Over the years, so many of our favorite celebrities have stepped out in the lace dress trend. From red carpets to streetwear, these stars are showing us how to rock lacy outfits to perfection.

The Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show is the perfect place to rock a lacy look. Since the fabric is reminiscent of lingerie, it goes great with the event’s pink carpet. Halsey performed at the VS show in 2018, and she absolutely slayed in her after-party look. Her dress was sheer with a lacy, corset-style bodice, and she paired the look with sky-high heels that wrapped around her ankle with a bow. She even wore a mesh headpiece to complete the super sexy outfit.

Kris Jenner is always quite the fashionista, and she knows how to rock the lace trend like no other! While attending an upfront for E!, she was red hot in a lacy mini dress, which she paired with matching, pointy-toed shoes. The momager also rocked a pop of bright red lipstick to match, and she was all smiles as she confidently posed in the outfit.

Obviously, Kris has passed her great sense of style onto her daughters. Kendall Jenner has also been seen out and about in lace. For a night out, she rocked a gold dress with pink, lacy trim. She paired the look with light eyeshadow and light pink lip gloss to match the color of the lace. The slip dress was a super sexy way for Kendall to keep comfortable and still look fabulous while hitting the town.
