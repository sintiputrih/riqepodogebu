His Siblings, Age &amp; Bio

Kross Ermias Asghedom is the son of the late rapper Nipsey Hussle and actress Lauren London. He was born on August 31, 2016, in Los Angeles, California, and his zodiac sign is Virgo.

Asghedom has already garnered a lot of popularity, especially among his father's fans. His mother, London, who also has a big fan following herself, has only contributed more to her son's fame and success.

Death Of Asghedom's Father, Nipsey Hussle

Asghedom is both fortunate and unfortunate at the same time. He is lucky because of he is born to famous parents and unfortunate at the same time because he will never get to see his father. Asghedom was only two years old when his father, Nipsey Hussle, was killed.

Asghedom's father, Nipsey, died from a gunshot which is also one of the most common reasons for death among American hip-hop artists. It's sad to see someone getting killed, especially when they are still young and have a lot of life ahead of them. He was only 33 years old at the time of his death.

Eric R. Holder, the man responsible for Asghedom's father's death, was found guilty three years after the incident occurred. The incident happened on March 31, 2019, in South Los Angeles, near his clothing store.

Asghedom's mother, London, has spoken to the media regarding her boyfriend's death since. She talked to the E! News about her healing process and how she has moved on with her life. In the same interview, she also talked about carrying his legacy forward. She said:

In my life now, anything that I do I want it to have purpose and I want it to be intentional. I hope that he would be proud that I am taking a chance on myself. Any and everything I do will always be attributed to him in my heart.

Nipsey Hussle Previous Relationship With Tanisha Foster

Let the Darkness In rapper Nipsey's relationship with Asghedom's mother is well-known to the public, but that wasn't the first time he found love. He was involved with Tanisha Foster. And it wasn't just a fling because their relationship lasted for years. 

The two were together from July 2003 till August 2011. Tanisha usually stays away from the media, but her custody battle with the Racks In The Middle rapper for their daughter was the talk of the town when it happened.

About Asghedom's Parent Relationship 

Kross Ermias Asghedom's parent's relationship was adored by many. They looked good together, and it's sad to learn how that relationship ended. So, how did it start between the two?

Reports suggest that the ex-couple Nipsey Hussle and Lauren London met in Los Angeles. The pair first started seeing each other in 2013. However, their relationship wasn't all rosy. 

Lauren and Nipsey were rumored to have been engaged as there were engagement photos of them. Later it was clarified that the pictures were only for the GQ photoshoot.

Essence reports that these two broke up for a while in November 2017 before patching things up. And the Always and 4Ever actress has acknowledged that their relationship wasn't always happy.

London talked about it while sitting down to speak in Angie Martinez's IRL Podcast. She had this to say about her relationship with the Grinding All My Life rapper:

We had pure love but that does not mean that our egos did not get attached in aspects of our relationship. But we definitely had pure love, always good intention.

Asghedom's Mother Was Previously Engaged To Lil Wayne

It's not just Kross Ermias Asghedom's father with a bit of a relationship history but also his mother. It looks like Madea's Big Happy Family actress, Lauren London, had a thing for hip-hop artists. Before Nipsey, she was in a relationship with the famous rapper Lil Wayne. 

London's relationship with the How To Love rapper Wayne went through many ups and downs, lasting from 1998 to 2009. The childhood sweethearts even shared a son together, Cameron Carter. We were in a relationship that didn't make it. We tried more than once to revive it, and we were engaged briefly years ago, but we eventually parted ways. My son's father is an intelligent, loving, and lovable person who will always be a dear friend. That is all.
