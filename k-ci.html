Biography

K-Ci was born in Monroe, NC on September 2, 1969. Widely known for comprising one-half of the iconic R&B duo C-Ci & JoJo alongside his brother Joel. He rose to prominence by guesting on 2Pac‘s 1997 hit single “How Do U Want It,” which topped the Billboard Hot 100 chart of that year. His fifth studio album My Brother’s Keeper was executive produced by fellow R&B icon R. Kelly. He first gained public attention for his cover of the hit single “If You Think You’re Lonely Now” for the 1994 film Jason’s Lyric.
