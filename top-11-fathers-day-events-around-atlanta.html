Top 11 Father's Day events around Atlanta

Looking for the perfect spot to take your Dad-of-the-year for Father's Day? Make Dad proud by giving him a day he won't forget and the family will enjoy.

Looking for the perfect spot to take your Dad of the year for Father's Day?

Make Dad proud by giving him a day he won't forget and the family will enjoy.

Check out the list below of the top 11 fun-filled events happening around Atlanta.


Reference Guide:

Father's Day at the Zoo
All Dads will receive free admission with purchase of any full-price child's or adult's general admission ticket. Valid on June 15, 2015 only.

06/15/2015
Zoo Atlanta
800 Cherokee Avenue, Atlanta, GA 30315
404-624-5600
www.zooatlanta.org

Atlanta Braves vs New York Mets
Cheer on the Atlanta Braves as they play against the New York Mets. Multi-platinum group Boyz II Men will perform live at Turner Field for a postgame concert

06/19/2015 - 06/21/2015
Turner Field
755 Hank Aaron Drive, Atlanta, GA 30302
404-522-7630
www.atlanta.braves.mlb.com

Honoring Our Fathers
Morehouse ATL Alumni present the 5th annual Honoring Our Fathers Awards Breakfast and Fundraiser. The event will celebrate fatherhood, mentorship and service.

06/20/2015
9:00 a.m.
200 Peachtree Grand Atrium
200 Peachtree Street NW, Atlanta, GA 30303
www.honoringourfathers.com

The 2015 Atlanta Summer Beer Fest
Take Dad to one of the best beer festivals of the summer. The Atlanta Summer Beer Fest will have an outdoor venue that will include over 200 beers, featuring the local Atlanta Breweries and great breweries from all over the country. This is a 21 and up event only.

06/20/2015
4:00 p.m. – 9:00 p.m.
Masquerade
695 North Ave Northeast, Atlanta, GA
404-577-8178
www.atlantasummerbeerfest.com

Father's Day at the Aquarium
Free admission for Dads with a purchase of one regular priced adult, senior or child's admission at the Georgia Aquarium and special gifts on Father's Day.

06/21/2015
Georgia Aquarium
225 Baker Street, Atlanta, GA 30313
404-581-4000
www.georgiaaquarium.org

Father's Day Car Show
Take Dad to a cool vintage car show on Father's Day to view classics such as Chevys, Camaros, Mustangs and more!

06/21/2015
10:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m.
Stone Mountain Park
1000 Robert E. Lee Boulevard, Stone Mountain, GA 30083
800-401-2407
www.stonemountainpark.com

Dad's Eat Free at Shula's
Dad gets a free Father's Day meal at Shula's 347 Grill.

06/21/2015
11:00 a.m. - 10:00 p.m.
Shula's 347 Grill - Marriott Atlanta Buckhead Hotel & Conference Center
3405 Lenox Road NE, Atlanta, GA 30326
404-848-7301
www.donshula.com

Family Food Festival
This multicultural Father's Day affair will feature more than 100 dishes from celebrity chefs, cooks, caterers, restaurants and everyday dads or men who serve in mentor roles for youth. There will also be live entertainment and a kids zone.

06/21/2015
3:00 p.m. – 6:00 p.m.
Georgia Aquarium
225 Baker Street, Atlanta, GA 30313
404-581-4000
www.familyfoodfestatlanta.com

Atlanta Dream vs New York Liberty
Cheer on the Atlanta Dream as they go against the New York Liberty.

06/21/2105
3:00 p.m.
Philips Arena
1 Philips Drive, Atlanta, GA 30303
404-878-3000
www.philipsarena.com

Dad's Eat Free at Dantanna's Downtown
Dads eat free on Father's Day at Dantanna's Downtown with a purchase of any menu item at regular price to receive a complimentary item of equal or lesser value.

06/21/2015
Dantanna's Downtown
190 Marietta Street NW, Ste. 269 Atlanta, GA 30303
404-522-8873
www.dantannas.com

Complimentary Pint for Dad
All dads will receive at free pint during dining with their families for Father's Day.

06/21/2015
TAP
1180 Peachtree Street, Atlanta, GA 30309
404-347-2220
