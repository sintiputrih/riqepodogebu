Shoppers With Receding Hairlines Saw Growth Thanks to This Shampoo

The thing about hair loss treatments is that as necessary as they might be for you (or me), it’s another step to add to your routine. It’s built into your shower routine and can become so second nature you’ll figure you're doing it. The Paisle Thickening Shampoo with 4,000-plus five-star ratings is originally $25, but thanks to a sale (with a coupon on top!), it’s 50 percent off. Some hair growth shampoos are meant to be cycled in your routine but according to the brand, this one is gentle enough to be used during every wash even if that means every day.

Paisle Biotin Thickening Shampoo

The key to this formula’s efficacy is the titular ingredient biotin (vitamin B7). But biotin’s efficacy is debatable at best, so it’s likely the non-spotlighted ingredients (keratin, rosemary oil, and saw palmetto) that are causing shoppers to dub this a “miracle worker.” Keratin has been shown to thicken strands and improve their strength, too. Rosemary is a hail mary for hair growth - one study found that its benefits are comparable to minoxidil which is an FDA-approved ingredient. It also contains carnosic acid which, according to one study, is an anti-inflammatory that repairs nerve damage which makes follicles healthier and improves hair growth. Lastly, saw palmetto may block DHT (dihydrotestosterone) a hormone that accelerates hair loss. 

“I noticed as I got into my sixties, that my hair was thinning rapidly,” a reviewer wrote. “[Paisle Shampoo] has helped to thicken my hair and reduce hair loss.” Another shopper said that after a few weeks of use, they saw “a tenth of the amount of hair in the drain after washing [their] hair” than usual. They also said, “I have new hair growth where it was previously receding.”
