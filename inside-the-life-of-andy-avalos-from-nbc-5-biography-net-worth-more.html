Who is Andy Avalos?

On 10th July 1956 Andy Avalos was born in the United States of America. He’s a veteran meteorologist popularly known for having worked at NBC5 News. What is Andy Avalos’s profession?

He began his weather forecasting career in the 1970s in Colorado Springs at the NBC affiliate. In 1977, he started working for a country radio station in the state of Colorado. He came to NBC in January of 1994 through 1999, before leaving to begin his own local business. In 2003, he returned to NBC and has been on the air ever since. After 33 years as a weather forecaster in Chicago, NBC 5’s Avalos eventually retired on August 27, 2021. Meteorologist Alicia Roman however took over the role as a primary meteorologist for NBC 5 News Today from 4 to 7 am. beginning Monday, Aug 30, 2021.
