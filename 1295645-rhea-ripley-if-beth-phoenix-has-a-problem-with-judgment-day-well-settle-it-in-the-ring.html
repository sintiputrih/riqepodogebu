Rhea Ripley: If Beth Phoenix Has A Problem With Judgment Day, Well Settle It In The Ring

welcomes a potential match with Beth Phoenix.

At WrestleMania Backlash, the Judgment Day team of Damian Priest and Edge grew in numbers as Rhea Ripley joined them and helped Edge score a second win against AJ Styles. On Twitter, Beth Pheonix, Edge’s wife, appeared to show her displeasure.

Not her ?

— THE GLAMAZON (@TheBethPhoenix) May 9, 2022

Speaking to 99.5 WKDQ, Ripley explained that she hasn’t spoken to Beth Pheonix about her joining  Judgment Day alongside Edge and Damian Priest, but despite her reservations, Beth Phoenix will have to fall in line and accept Judgment Day or face “The Eradicator” instead.

“I haven’t spoken to Beth, but I have been seeing a lot of posts on Twitter. I don’t know if she’s very happy about me joining Judgment Day. So Beth gonna have to put her emotions aside and be on our side pretty much or she’s gonna have to fight me,” said Ripley. “Beth Phoenix is my dream opponent. And really, if she disagrees with what we’re saying, I wouldn’t mind stepping in the ring with her and settling it once and for all.”

In her promo on WWE Raw this past Monday, Ripley said she joined Judgment Day because her partnership with Liv Morgan made her weak. Learn more here.
