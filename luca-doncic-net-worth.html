Luka Doncic | Career

Starting his basketball journey at the age of eight, the man has successfully paved the way for his success.

In 2012, Doncic signed a contract with Real Madrid and led Madrid to a Minicopa Endesa win.

Similarly, in 2014-15 he played for the Under-18 team. He started his professional career in 2015 after making his debut with Real Madrid in the Liga ACB.

Finally, in 2015-16, he became a regular player of Real Madrid’s senior team and debuted in the Euroleague, which made him the first player to do so before turning 17.

In 2016-17 he was named Euroleague Rising Star because of his excellent performance against many proficient teams.

Moreover, in 2018, he was selected as the third overall pick by the Atlanta Hawks but was later traded to Dallas Mavericks.
