KYLIE JENNER AND TRAVIS SCOTT GAVE THEIR SON, WOLF, A MIDDLE NAME THAT HONORS DAD

Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott’s son, Wolf, has a middle name that honors his dad. The newborn’s moniker is the same as his father’s legal name.

Travis’ birth name is Jacques Webster, which is the middle name Wolf carries. Kylie and Travis reportedly chose to refrain from giving their daughter, Stormi, a middle name. It appears the celebrity couple is changing things with their second child.

Wolf Webster was born on February 2, 2022. Kylie and Travis welcomed their son one day after celebrating their daughter’s fourth birthday. Travis shared how fatherhood has transformed his life during an interview last year.

“Fatherhood influences my job,” Travis shared with i-D last winter. “It has a huge impact,” the rapper added. “It’s a major inspiration,” Travis told the press.

Stormi Webster made both Travis and Kylie parents for the first time in 2018. The celebrities were rumored to be expecting a child for months before revealing the birth of their daughter on her birthdate.

“I shared so much of my life,” Kylie recently said when explaining why she chose to keep her first pregnancy out of the public’s eye. “I was also really young when I got pregnant, and it was just a lot for me personally,” the businesswoman explained.

“I didn’t know how I would bring that to the public too and have everyone’s opinion,” Jenner added. “I think it was just something that I needed to go through by myself.”

Stormi Webster has been the inspiration for growth for both her mom and dad. “Motherhood really feels like something I was always meant to do,” Kylie Jenner recently said during an interview with ELLE. “Watching Stormi experience everything for the first time has been the best part of these last few years,” the celebrity mom shared.
