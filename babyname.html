Domenico - Baby Name Meaning, Origin, and Popularity

Romantic and artistic Domenico is ripe for the picking as a relatively rare name that fits in with current favorites such as Leonardo and Emiliano.

Domenico is the name of many great creatives throughout history, including many painters, composers, and sculptors. In modern times, Domenico has been associated with designer Domenico Dolce, cofounder of fashion house Dolce & Gabbana.
