Jazian Gortman: Ready To Takeover Basketball

Hailing from South Carolina, Jazian Gortman is a name that is fast becoming synonymous with skill, perseverance, and dedication in the world of professional basketball. His journey from high school basketball to the Overtime Elite (OTE) league and his recent signing of an NBA Exhibit 10 Deal is a testament to his talent and hard work.

Early Days and High School Career

Born and raised in Columbia, South Carolina, Gortman's love for basketball began at an early age. He spent his days honing his skills at the local gym and playing basketball at every opportunity he got. He attended W.J. Keenan High School, where he was a key player in the school's basketball team. As a junior, Gortman led the Raiders in scoring with an impressive average of 23.1 points per game and was a major contributor to the team's success.

Moving to the Professional League

In what was to be his senior year at Keenan High School, Gortman made a life-altering decision. He chose to forgo his high school senior season and instead joined the Overtime Elite league. This move was driven by his ambition to prepare for a full-time career in the NBA. Despite missing the camaraderie of his high school team, Gortman embraced the professional environment of the OTE league.

Overtime Elite: A New Chapter

Overtime Elite, an upstart professional league, offers an alternative path for aspiring basketball players. By joining OTE, Gortman took a significant step towards his dream of making it to the NBA. At OTE, he had the opportunity to train, compete, and learn under the guidance of seasoned professionals.

The OTE league is based in Atlanta and offers its players a minimum salary of $100,000 per year, along with bonuses, equity shares in Overtime, and revenue from jersey sales, trading cards, and video games. This was a significant incentive for Gortman, who reportedly earned $650,000 in his first year with OTE.

Standout Performances and NBA Aspirations

In his debut season with OTE, Gortman quickly established himself as a player to watch. He averaged 24.4 points, seven assists, and 6.3 rebounds per game. His energy, athleticism, and leadership skills earned him praise from his coaches and peers.

Gortman's outstanding performances caught the attention of NBA scouts. Many predict that he will be a top pick in the NBA draft when he becomes eligible. He recently signed an NBA Exhibit 10 Deal, taking him a step closer to his NBA dream.

Why Gortman Deserves to be on an Active NBA Roster

Gortman's skillset, coupled with his relentless work ethic, make him a deserving candidate for an active NBA roster. His explosive energy, scoring ability, and defensive prowess set him apart from his peers. Moreover, his experience with OTE has prepared him for the professional environment of the NBA.

His decision to join OTE over traditional college basketball has proven to be a wise one. It has allowed him to focus on his game, develop pro habits, and compete at a high level. He has embraced the professional lifestyle, dedicating himself to constant improvement and learning the nuances of the game.

Looking Ahead: A Promising Future

Gortman's journey from a high school player to a professional athlete is inspiring. His determination, coupled with his natural talent, makes him a player to watch in the coming years. His recent NBA Exhibit 10 Deal with the Milwaukee Bucks is just the beginning of what promises to be a successful career in professional basketball.

We are excited to see where Gortman's journey takes him. With his talent, perseverance, and passion for the game, there's no doubt that he will make a significant impact in the NBA. His story serves as a reminder that with hard work and determination, dreams can become reality.

In conclusion, Jazian Gortman is not just a rising star in the world of basketball. He is a testament to the power of hard work, determination, and a dream. His journey is far from over, and the world is excited to see where his talent takes him next.
