Why Dylan O'Brien's Alleged Girlfriend Rachael Lange Flies Under The Radar

Highlights

  • Rachael Lange keeps a private life despite being a model and allegedly dating Dylan O'Brin.
  • Lange remains active on social media but has limited comments after her Tweet scandal.
  • Although they haven't confirmed their relationship publicly, Dylan O'Brien and Rachael Lange have been spotted together.

Dylan O'Brien may be famous for TV shows like Teen Wolf and the movies Not Okay and The Maze Runner, but the actor has dated a lot, and fans are always interested in his juicy love life. O'Brien was in a rumored relationship with Sabrina Carpenter. It's also possible that O'Brien had another private relationship with Sarah Ramos.

In the past little while, Dylan O'Brien has been linked to model Rachael Lange. However, while her alleged boyfriend is a public figure, Lange seems to be living more privately than ever. Here's all there is to know about her life today and why she is keeping her distance from the public.

Why Does Rachael Lange Live Such A Private Life?

Rachael Lange has been working as a model for a while now, and she is still signed with Elite Models NYC, according to the company's official website. Since she works in a public-facing industry and seems to be dating a famous actor, people might expect her to be everywhere. However, that isn't the case.

It's possible that Rachael Lange is making sure to stay away from the spotlight because she tweeted some controversial things, which got her a lot of attention. According to The Tab, Lange tweeted several racist remarks in the time period between 2013 and 2016.

While Lange never gave any interviews about the tweets, she did make an official statement where she shared that she knew that she was wrong. She said, “I would like to wholeheartedly apologise for past tweets of mine. I am mortified and ashamed that they exist; and am truly disgusted that I ever spoke that way."

She continued,

"All I can say is that I hear you all and am sorry for the pain I’ve caused and contributed to. I want to make it abundantly clear that I take fully responsibility. I was extremely ignorant and while I do not remotely resonate with the things I once said, there is zero justification for my ever having said them. I understand the significance that my words have and am deeply sorry for the hurt I have caused.”

People found out about these tweets when, in January 2023, Lange and Dylan O'Brien were at Paris Fashion Week and it was clear that they might be dating.

O'Brien doesn't seem to have commented on what his alleged girlfriend tweeted, either at the time or since then.

In the months since then, no one has heard much from Rachael Lange. While no one can be sure unless she explains, it's definitely possible that she wanted to take a time-out after this happened.

Lange still seems to be modeling, and she is still active on social media. However, while she has 181,000 followers on TikTok, she hasn't posted since December 15th, 2022. It's possible that she hasn't wanted to share anything on the platform because she doesn't want to see any negative comments. There are several comments on the most recent post from December 2022 that mention her tweets and the fact that she is allegedly in a relationship with O'Brien.

While Lange is more active on Instagram and has been posting regularly throughout 2023, she only shares selfies or photos of herself, and she doesn't write much in the captions. She has also limited comments. It's tough to see what she has been up to based on this account. Since Lange is a model, she seems to be totally comfortable in front of the camera, but she might not want to talk to the media in case people mention what she tweeted in the past.

Are Rachael Lange And Dylan O'Brien Still Dating?

Rachael Lange and Dylan O'Brien have never appeared to confirm their romance. But they definitely seem to still be dating.

In early October 2023, someone posted in the FauxMoi SubReddit about the relationship and titled the post "New photo of Dylan O’Brien and gf Rachael Lange at NYC wedding." The fan wrote, "Dylan’s gf posted this on her IG account today." Lange had included a picture of O'Brien twirling her around on her Instagram Stories.

Several people commented that they didn't like what she tweeted. One Redditor shared a rumor that they had heard: "Also not sure how accurate this is but according to the Swifties, the reason why he hasnt been seen around (I don't think he's been to her concert) is because RL hates Taylor." Of course, this is just speculation and it hasn't been confirmed. However, someone posted out on Reddit that Lange did tweet an insult about Taylor Swift several years back.

Since Dylan O'Brien is pretty private about his relationship with Rachael Lange, his fanbase often questions whether they have broken up.

Also earlier in October 2023, a fan posted in the FauxMoi SubReddit and asked "Dylan O’Brien no longer dating Rachael Lange?" This was based on a comment DeuxMoi posted on their Instagram Stories from a fan that said "Camila Mendes & Dylan O'Brien partied together on Sat. They'd be a cute couple!"

Several fans commented that O'Brien and Mendes are likely pals because the actor still seems to be dating Lange. As one Redditor wrote, "Rachael Lange just posted photos from Dylan’s apartment. She does photoshoots in his place. He’s been with her for almost a year now."

While Dylan O'Brien hasn't spoken publicly about Rachael Lange, he is open about his love life. The actor answered questions about dating in a 2022 interview with Glamour. He said that he likes "clear communication" and isn't a fan of "making the move" and "having to read the moment."

He added,

"I will say that my crush, like a few months ago, did kiss me, like, midsentence, and it was really cool. But that's a very specific situation. So I do get the flare of it, but for the most part, an even playing field is more comfortable…and not surprising women with kisses."

It's unclear who O'Brien was talking about at the time, but it sounds like he was happy. It's possible that if he and Lange continue to date, they will still keep their romance out of the limelight. It seems to be a choice that they wanted to make.
