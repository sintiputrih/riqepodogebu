Andr Burakovsky Wife: Is Andr Burakovsky Married?

Andre Burakovsky is currently not married.

The Austrian-Swedish professional ice hockey player was born on February 9, 1995, in Klagenfurt, Austria to Robert Burakovsky and Pernilla Burakovsky.

His father Robert Burakovsky is a Swedish professional ice hockey coach and former player. He was drafted 217th overall 1985 NHL Entry Draft and played 23 games with the Ottawa Senators. While his mother Pernilla was a housewife.

Andre Burakovsky has two sisters; Alexandra Burakovsky and Anna Burakovsky.

Known for his flashy offensive game, Burakovsky was selected in the third round, 102nd overall by SKA Saint Petersburg in the 2012 KHL Junior Draft, and was drafted 23rd overall by the Washington Capitals at the 2013 NHL Entry Draft.

The 27-year-old Andre Burakovsky currently plays forward for the Seattle Kraken of the National Hockey League (NHL).

He has previously played for the Washington Capitals and Colorado Avalanche.

Burakovsky is a two-time Stanley Cup champion, winning with the Capitals in 2018 and the Avalanche in 2022.
