Al Pacino Spotted with Ex amid Girlfriend's Pregnancy They Are Still Close despite Split

Al Pacino and Beverly D'Angelo | Noor Alfallah and Al Pacino | Source: Getty Images | Instagram.com/@nooralfallah

  • Legendary Hollywood actor Al Pacino was recently spotted out with his ex-partner and mother of his twins, Beverly D'Angelo, amidst his current girlfriend being eight months pregnant with his fourth child.
  • D'Angelo met Pacino on a plane 27 years ago, where he professed that he wanted her to be the mother of his children one day.
  • The ex-couple broke up in 2004 for reasons described as "complicated."

On Sunday afternoon, paparazzi spotted Al Pacino hanging out with his ex-flame, American actress Beverly D'Angelo. The ex-couple was seen enjoying a family fun day with their son Anton James Pacino as they made their way to an escape room in Los Angeles. The family outing comes only a week after news had just broken concerning Pacino's current 29-year-old girlfriend being eight months pregnant with his fourth child.

As the family enjoyed spending time together, photographs showed a chicly dressed D'Angelo and a relaxed-looking Pacino in the middle of what looked to be a serious discussion. After wrapping up their conversation, the ex-couple proceeded to enter the escape room with their family.

What the Beverly D'Angelo and Al Pacino's Family Reunion Looked Like

Pictures of the ex-partners reuniting were splayed across every major news outlet. The family reunion was an all-black attire affair: D'Angelo adorned a black and white pinstriped t-shirt paired with black slacks, a black blazer, and black sneakers. Patriarch Pacino was clad in a black shirt, black joggers, and a black coat to match, while his son similarly wore a black shirt and black shorts with black New Balance shoes.

As the family continued to be photographed, viewers noticed how deeply engaged and happy Pacino appeared to be when communicating with his ex, even while the two were seated and involved in a serious chat at one point. When giving a recent account of their relationship history, D'Angelo boasted about how she and Pacino remain friends who share a unique bond that surpasses a traditional relationship to this day.

The "Violent Night" talent went on about how she and her ex have always been connected via a kinship with creative pursuits that has existed since the 90s. Apart from the special family moment, a new family member was noticeably absent from the festivities - Pacino's new flame, Noor Alfallah, who is set to welcome his fourth child later this year.

Where It All Started: Beverly D'Angelo and Al Pacino's Relationship History

Pacino and D'Angelo's history has been filled with eventful feats that involved many ups and downs before leading to a split in 2004. On May 8, D'Angelo took to her Instagram to give fans some details about the relationship she shared/s with Pacino. According to D'Angelo, her DM inbox was getting flooded with all sorts of questions from fans wanting to know more about her romantic timeline with the "Scarface" actor.

Deciding to indulge the requests, D'Angelo began the story that showcased heartwarmingly nostalgic photos and videos of her, Pacino, and their twins as she narrated their history as a family. The ex-lovebirds met 27 years ago, in 1996, on a plane going from Los Angeles to New York. As soon as Pacino noticed D'Angelo, he knew she would one day become the mother of his future children.

Pacino's recent appearance with his ex-partner D'Angelo was all about enjoying some family fun with their kids as friendly co-parents.

Al Pacino and Beverly D'Angelo at "The Insider" premiere in New York City, 1999 | Source: Getty Images

The scene was set on the plane, Pacino was seated in front of D'Angelo, and she was sitting behind him. While continuing to tell the story, D'Angelo recalled:

"He was seated in front of me, [and] he said come up and sit beside me, and by the time the plane landed, it was on. In 1997, he looked me in the eye and said, 'I want you to be the mother of my children.' "

D'Angelo admits that she had avoided becoming a mother all her life, but she was deeply in love with Pacino and committed to the actor 100%. Thus, their journey to parenthood began, and in 1997, they underwent their first round of IVF, and after a few attempts, the couple became parents to twins - Anton James and Olivia Rose Pacino in 2001.

Beverly D'Angelo and Al Pacino spotted in Los Angeles, 2001 | Source: Getty Images

After seven years of living together as a happy family, things became complicated, and the two ended up going their separate ways in 2004. However, compelled by the power of the love they share for their twins, Pacino and D'Angelo put their quarrels to the side. They focused on becoming friends in order to raise their children to the best of their abilities as co-parents:

"Yikes, it got complicated, and our split was finalized in 2004. The power of our love for our children was the basis for resolving any conflicts and creating a new history as co-parents. Leading individual lives separately but always intertwined as a family."

She ends off the beautiful story by giving an update on where she and Pacino currently stand today. D'Angelo disclosed that she and her ex share a uniquely profound friendship that is enduring and continues to blossom through thick and thin.

The 'Who' behind Al Pacino's 29-Year-Old Girlfriend and Mother to His Fourth Child, Noor Alfallah

Shifting gears from the past to the present, Alfallah is Pacino's current partner, and the two have been linked to each other since April 2022. The soon-to-be mother to Pacino's fourth child is no stranger to the limelight, or the glitz and glam of Tinseltown, for that matter, as she works as a producer in the industry herself.

After completing her undergrad at the University of Southern California's Cinematic School of Arts and graduating with a Master's in film/TV production at UCLA, Alfallah has already produced three productions. Her IMDb profile denotes the three accredited productions of "Brosa Nostra," "La Petite Mort," and "Billy Knight," which also stars her famous boyfriend, Pacino.

Apart from her career, her dating history shows she has been no stranger to engaging in romantic relationships with men who are older. Before dating Pacino, she was romantically involved with The Rolling Stones frontman Mick Jagger when she was 22, and he was 74. Following this relationship, she dated billionaire Nicolas Berggruen in that same year.

Alfallah also comes from a wealthy family and is the eldest of four. She has two younger sisters and a brother who all grew up in Beverly Hills. She appears to be big on family, as her Instagram page is peppered with endearing photos of her siblings and parents.
