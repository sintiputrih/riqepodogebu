The Rookie's Jenna Dewan is all legs as she details relatable Halloween aftermath

Many parents may be ready for all of the Halloween shenanigans to be over, Jenna Dewan included, but she is still taking a moment to show off her look!

The star dressed her whole family up, but while her children certainly looked cute, she stole the show in her mini get-up.
