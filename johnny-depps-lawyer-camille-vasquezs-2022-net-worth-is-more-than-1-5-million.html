Who Is Johnny Depp’s Lawyer Camille Vasquez?

Camille Vasquez, a 37-year-old Virginia lawyer, has a long history in the courtroom.

As per her bio on Brown Rudnick’s profile: She is an expert in “plaintiff-side defamation actions,” as well as contract disputes, corporate torts, and employment-related claims.

According to her LinkedIn, Vasquez had joined Brown Rudnick in the year 2018. Before these, she worked at the Manatt, Phelps & Phillips, LLP for more than three years.
