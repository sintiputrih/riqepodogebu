FTR Takes Top Spot In 2023 PWI Tag Team Top 100

The Top Guys are on top.

AEW’s Dax Harwood and Cash Wheeler, collectively known as FTR, have taken the top spot in Pro Wrestling Illustrated’s annual Tag Team 100. This comes one year after the duo ranked #2 behind The Usos (Jimmy and Jey Uso) in 2022.

Harwood and Wheeler won the AEW World Team Championship for the second time earlier in 2022. They are also former ROH, IWGP, and AAA World Tag Team Champions. Before their arrival in AEW, Harwood and Wheeler were WWE’s first Tag Team Triple Crown winners.

The top ten of the 2023 PWI Tag Team 100 is as follows:

  • 1. FTR (Dax Harwood & Cash Wheeler)
  • 2. Aussie Open (Kyle Fletcher & Mark Davis)
  • 3. Sami Zayn & Kevin Owens
  • 4. Bishamon (YOSHI-HASHI & Hirooki Goto)
  • 5. Motor City Machine Guns (Alex Shelley & Chris Sabin)
  • 6. ABC (Ace Austin & Chris Bey)
  • 7. The Acclaimed (Anthony Bowens & Max Caster)
  • 8. Judgement Day (Finn Balor, Damian Priest & Dominik Mysterio)
  • 9. Damage CTRL (Dakota Kai, Bayley & IYO SKY)
  • 10. 7UPP (Yuu & Nanae Takahashi)
