About

Los Angeles-based Actress turned YouTube star who creates online content of a comedic nature for her self-titled YouTube channel, which has earned over 1.2 million subscribers. She also has two other YouTube channels where she posts vlogs, behind the scenes bloopers, baking and cooking tutorials and more. 

Before Fame

She uploaded a parody music video and song to her YouTube account in 2009 titled "MILEY CYRUS Party In The USA Everything is Gonna Be Okay" which went viral with over six million views. 

Trivia

She has created branded content for major brands like Lionsgate, GE, Panasonic, Hewlett Packard, Kodak, Wonderful Pistachios, and more.

Family Life

She grew up with a brother. In mid-2016, she became engaged to her boyfriend Milos. Later that same year, she and Milos welcomed a son named Misha. She and Milos later broke up.

Associated With

She has collaborated multiple times with YouTube star Ricky Dillon.
