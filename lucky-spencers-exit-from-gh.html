Lucky Spencer's exit from GH - General Hospital

Lucky left Port Charles after the death of his wife Siobhan Mckenna. He found a letter from her asking him to go to a church in Ireland. While he was there, he had visions of her telling him that Aiden was sick. Lucky called Luke and asked him to check on Aiden. Luke to him to the hospital. Lucky had been working undercover in Ireland. He looked like Siobhan’s boyfriend. I won’t go into detail, but it goes back to the Balkin. He wanted Lucky to kill Brenda Barrett.
