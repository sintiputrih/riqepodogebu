Who are Zach and Kaity?

Kaity Biggar

Kaitlyn “Kaity” Biggar, who is 27 and from Kingston, Ontario, Canada, now lives in Austin, Texas, where Zach also lives. Her Instagram name is @kaityylane.

Zach Shallcross, who was on The Bachelorette season 19 with Gabby Windey and Rachel Recchia, will be the Bachelor for season 27. Zach was Rachel’s contestant, and in the final three, he chose to leave on his own. Are Zach and Kaity Still Together After The Bachelor 2023?

Yes, that’s the answer. Zach chose Kaity as his winner at The Bachelor season 27’s Final Rose event in Krabi, Thailand. He gave her his last rose and asked her to marry him.

In the “After the Final Rose” show for season 27 of “The Bachelor,” Zach and Kaity said that they were still together. “When I saw her at the last chance date, I thought, ‘It’s you. You’ve always been the one. “I love this woman, and I want to spend the rest of my life with her,” Zach said.”Because of the show, I had to wait a little bit and couldn’t say much, but I just knew she was the one.”
