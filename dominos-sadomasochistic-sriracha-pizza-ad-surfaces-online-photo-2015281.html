Domino's Sadomasochistic Sriracha Pizza Ad Surfaces Online: Photo

OMG, make it go away. An ad for Domino’s Sriracha Pizza surfaced online from Israel and it’s terrifying. In an attempt to sell their “super spicy” pizza, one of the world’s largest pizza chains jumped on the Fifty Shades of Grey bandwagon and featured a tongue in the ad, gagged and bound in a leather get-up. Surrounding the tongue? A bunch of equally terrifying-looking sex toys.

The print ad, which is rumored to be running in Israel reads, “You’re going to suffer and enjoy every moment.” 

But there’s a catch. After hitting the web — and naturally making headlines — Domino’s tweeted that the ad is actually fake.

“@ToplessRobot Thanks for reaching out. This was created with unauthorized use of our branding. The people responsible are being dealt with,” Domino’s wrote to a follower.
