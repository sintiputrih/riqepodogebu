Early Life, Family, Background, Siblings, Education and Religion

Emily Wickersham has Austrian and Swedish roots and grew up in Mamaroneck, New York, with her parents, Amy Kaiser and John Wickersham.

She also has a younger brother named Adam. She attended Muhlenberg College for two years before opting out.

She’s not overly religious but holds a belief in God.

Personal Life, Marriages, Relationships and Parenthood

Emily Wickersham tied the knot with musician and domestic violence lawyer Blake Hanley on November 23, 2010, at Little Palm Island in the Florida Keys.

However, they separated in December 2018 after eight years of marriage.

Presently, she’s dating actor James Badge Dale, recognized for his roles in The Departed, Iron Man 3, and The Lone Ranger.

They welcomed their first child, a son named Cassius Wickersham Dale, on December 30, 2021.

Her Career Path

Emily Wickersham embarked on her acting journey in 2006 with a guest appearance on the Late Show with David Letterman.

She took on recurring roles in TV series like The Sopranos and The Bronx Is Burning.

She also appeared in various films, including Gardener of Eden, Definitely, Maybe, How I Got Lost, Remember Me, I Am Number Four, and Gone.

In 2013, she joined the cast of NCIS as NSA Analyst Eleanor “Ellie” Bishop, taking over from Cote de Pablo’s character, Ziva David.

Initially meant for a three-episode arc, she later became a regular series member and assumed the role of an NCIS Special Agent for eight seasons.

She exited the show at the conclusion of season 18 in May 2021, with her character leaving for an undercover mission.

She also made a guest appearance in an episode of NCIS: New Orleans and played a role in the independent film Glitch.

Since departing from NCIS, she hasn’t announced any new projects.

Recognitions and Accomplishments Although Emily Wickersham hasn’t clinched any major awards, she received two People’s Choice Awards nominations for Favorite TV Crime Drama Actress in 2015 and 2016 for her NCIS role.

She’s garnered praise from both critics and fans for her portrayal of Ellie Bishop.
