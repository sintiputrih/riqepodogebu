Biography

Johnny Lozada was born in Caguas, PR on December 21, 1967. Puerto Rican singer, actor, and television personality is known for performing with the boy-band Menudo from 1980 to 1984. He has hosted Univision’s morning talk show called ¡Despierta América! He won the fourth season of Mira Quién Baila and went on to become a judge from there. Pop star Ricky Martin was also a member of Menudo. In addition to his membership in Menudo, he has also acted in the telenovelas Quiero Ser and Es for Amor.
