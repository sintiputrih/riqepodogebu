Emily Ratajkowski Dyes Her Hair a Warm Red for Summer

Emily Ratajkowski is now a fiery redhead.

The model debuted her new, warm red hair on TikTok today, simply sharing a video of herself dancing inside a New York City subway car while wearing a little black strapless dress. She finished the look with white sneakers with peach-colored soles and a green crochet tote bag by Prada. Her makeup was bronzy and included a pouty, glossy lip. Her new hair, meanwhile, was styled straight down, with her bangs casually resting above her brows.

“Not a filter,” she wrote over the clip. (The redhead filter on TikTok has been trending for some time, but EmRata’s hair is the real thing.)

“Soemtimes [sic] ya just need a change you kno?” she added in the caption.

Ratajkowski’s hair transformation comes just days after she showcased her originally brunette locks while on a dreamy getaway in France with baby son Sylvester. While there, the My Body author shared a fashion-filled photo dump on Instagram that included snaps with her beautiful baby boy, pictures of delicious meals, romantic sunsets, and dreamy landscapes, as well as photos of her vacation outfits.

In one photo, EmRata is seen standing in what looks to be a church cemetery wearing a frilly sheer brown dress. The completely see-through piece features spaghetti straps and breezy layers. Ratajkowski skipped the bra, wearing the midi dress only with a black thong that was visible under the thin sheer fabric. She finished the summer look with strappy light brown sandals, a brown leather shoulder bag, and skinny black sunglasses.
