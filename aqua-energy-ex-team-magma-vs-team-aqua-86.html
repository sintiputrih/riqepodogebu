Aqua Energy
アクアエネルギー Aqua Energy

Aqua Energy (Japanese: アクアエネルギー Aqua Energy) is a Special Energy card. It is part of the EX Team Magma vs Team Aqua expansion.

Card text

All prints
Aqua Energy can be attached only to a Pokémon with Team Aqua in its name. Aqua Energy provides and Energy but provides 2 Energy at a time. (Doesn't count as a basic Energy card when not in play and has no effect other than providing Energy.) At the end of your turn, discard Aqua Energy.

Release information

This card was included in the EX Team Magma vs Team Aqua expansion, first released in the Japanese Magma VS Aqua: Two Ambitions subset. It was also available in the Japanese Aqua Deck Kit.
