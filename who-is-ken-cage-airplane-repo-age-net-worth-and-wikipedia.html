Who Is Ken Cage Airplane Repo? Age Net Worth and Wikipedia

Who Is Ken Cage Airplane Repo? He joined the hit TV series in January 2013. Here is what we know about Ken so far.

Ken Cage is an American television celebrity who stars on the Discovery Channel’s Airplane Repo. He has starred on Airplane Repo for the past three seasons. He is joined by Danny Thompson, and they hunt and search, run-down boats, and airplanes for many banks and relieve debtors for their unpaid assets.

He is also the President of the International Recover Group (IRG), the world’s premiere Airplane Repossession company. They specialize in the investigation, recovery, and remarketing of specialty assets. Ken is often put in uncertain situations with his job, and he has several repossessions of assets to his credit.
