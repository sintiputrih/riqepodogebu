Pregnant Christina Anstead Looks Flawless in Makeup-Free Selfie, Admits Shes Been Crabby an

One sleepy mama-to-be! Christina Anstead looked flawless in a makeup-free selfie posted to her Instagram account on Wednesday, May 8, however, she revealed she didn’t feel so great.

“Swipe right for this amazing before and after @pinkdustcosmetics transformation 😜… but in all seriousness I’ve been feeling crabby and tired this week,” the HGTV star, 35, captioned two photos — one of her au-natural and another with a face full of cosmetics. “Grateful for my bestie for putting me together for work and our morning b** sessions that only besties would understand.”

Anstead received an outpour of support in the comments section of the post, with many followers praising her for sharing a sans makeup selfie.

“I love that you shared this! It’s nice to see the ‘real’ people behind the shows and I think it’s so helpful on social media to know that nobody looks made-up and tv ready 100% of the time,” one commenter wrote. Another added: “Beautiful both ways!! Natural and with pretty makeup! 💗 So glad you posted this!”

Anstead — who tied the knot with husband Ant Anstead at their California home in December 2018 — announced her pregnancy in a sweet Instagram post in March.
