The Weeknd popularly known as Abel Makkonen Tesfaye is a Canadian singer, songwriter and record producer. As of 2023, The Weeknd’s net worth is $300 million. He accumulated his net worth through his singing and songwriting career. He is popularly known for his distinctive singing voice with a soaring falsetto and its singular tremolo.

He is also popularly known for releasing several popular singles and albums like House of Balloons, Echoes of Silence, Kiss Land, Can’t Feel My Face, After Hours, and others. He has won several popular awards throughout his career in the industry.

The Weeknd Wiki/Biography

Born on 16 February 1990, The Weeknd’s age is 33 Years Old as of 2023. He was born and raised in a well-settled Christian family from Toronto, Ontario, Canada. He is a Canadian by nationality and has his belief in the Christian religion.

The Weeknd Childhood Pic

He completed his early schooling at West Hill Collegiate Institute and Birchmount Park Collegiate Institute in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.

He also smoked marijuana and started using ecstasy, oxycodone, Xanax, cocaine, psilocybin, and ketamine when he was just eleven years old.

Career

The Weeknd started his career as a YouTuber and released several music videos on his YouTube channel in the year 2009. In the year 2010, he has released three songs titled What You Need, Loft Music, and The Morning on his YouTube channel which he has recorded in collaboration with producer Jeremy Rose. Initially, he also worked at a popular American retailer company American Apparel.

In the year 2011, he co-founded a record label named XO along with Wassim Sal Slaiby, and Amir Cash Esmailian. In the same year, he released his debut mixtape titled House of Balloons. In August 2010, he released his second mixtape titled Thursday and in December his third mixtape was released titled Echoes of Silence.

He also performed at various live events in the same year. He has also contributed to the album titled Take Care by popular rapper and singer Drake and been featured in the song titled Crew Love from the same album.

In the year 2012, he released a compilation album titled Trilogy. In the year 2013, he released the title track from his debut studio album titled Kiss Land. In the same year, he has also contributed to the soundtrack from the movie titled The Hunger Games: Catching Fire.

In the year 2014, he remixed the single titled Drunk in Love by Beyonce. In the same year, he has collaborated with popular singer Ariana Grande for the song titled Love Me Harder from the album My Everything.

In the year 2015, he has released several singles like Often, King of the Fall, and Earned It. Along with his second studio album titled Beauty Behind the Madness. In the same year, he has released several singles from his second studio album like The Hills and Can’t Feel My Face. In June 2015, he has been featured in the track titled Pullin Up from the album Dreams Worth More Than Money by Meek Mill.

In September 2015, he has also been featured in the track Pray 4 Love from the album Rodeo by Travis Scott. In October 2015, he made his appearance on the popular television reality show Saturday Night Live and was featured in the song titled Might Not from mixtape Up For Days by Belly.
