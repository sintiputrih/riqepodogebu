Kim Kardashian Shares Photo of Saint West for His Birthday -

Kim Kardashian has slowly been teasing her family's advent calendar-style Christmas card, sharing a photo of one member of the Kardashian-Jenner clan each day. The latest image spotlights she and Kanye West's son, Saint, who turns two years old today. Kardashian shared the cutest photo of her little boy sporting a tiny pair of jeans and itty-bitty cornrows.

"HAPPY BIRTHDAY to my beautiful boy Saint! I love you so much! You don’t even understand!!!" his proud mom wrote on Instagram.

Saint's portrait only marks the fifth day of the Kardashian-Jenners' 25 Days of Christmas photo series, shot by Eli Russell Linnetz.

The first photo featured Saint from afar, amidst a pile of presents, with a sliver of a Christmas tree to the side:

The second starred North West making faces while her mom, Kim, and aunt, Kourtney Kardashian, posed behind her:

Then came this photo of Kourtney and Scott Disick's youngest child, two-year-old Reign:

The fourth was another picture of North, this time sharing a laugh with her silver-haired mom, while both dressed in white tank tops and denim:

We still have a lot more family members to see in the next 20 days, including Khloé Kardashian, Kourtney's other two kids, Penelope and Mason, Kylie Jenner, Kendall Jenner, Rob Kardashian, Caitlyn Jenner, and the momager herself, Kris Jenner. Even her mother, MJ, is slated to make a cameo. There's no word yet whether estranged exes like Scott Disick and Blac Chyna, or current beaus like Kanye West, will also make an appearance.

Kim organized the whole shoot, but she'll "never do it again,"she told Entertainment Tonight.

"Just to wrangle ever single family member was so exhausting," she continued. "I thought it would be really easy. It was really tough. But it'll be a good one this year, I think our best yet."
