How to cancel your Lifelock account

About LifeLock

Lifelock is an identity theft protection company based in America. Here are 7 steps to help you learn how to cancel your Lifelock subscription.

Some Account Information You Might Need

We’ve found that the following information might be needed when you use the below methods to cancel. First Name Last Name Phone Number Email Address Username Password Billing Address City State/Province/Region ZIP/Postal Code Country Reason for Cancellation Last 4 Digits of Card Date of Last Charge Amount of Last Charge Social Security Number The Security Question setup on your account The Answer to the Security Question setup on your account

How to Cancel LifeLock by Phone (Live Agent)

Step 1. Contact the business at 800-543-3562 Step 2. Pick the menu option number 1 and then number 4 Step 3. Advise the representative that you want to cancel your account Step 4. Provide the agent with your account info Step 5. Ask the representative if you will be charged again Step 6. Ask for a verbal confirmation code or email as proof of your cancellation Step 7. Retain the confirmation information you’re given
