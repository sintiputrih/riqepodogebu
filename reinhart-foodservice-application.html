Reinhart FoodService Application: Jobs &amp; Careers Online

Are you looking for a career in food distribution that will give you the chance to grow?

Then it’s time to consider applying to work at Reinhart FoodService. This is one of the leading foodservice distribution companies in the United States and was founded in 1950.

Today, Reinhart FoodService operates more than thirty locations in the United States. New employees are regularly hired to keep up with the growing demand from customers.

So, to make sure your Reinhart FoodService Application: Jobs & Careers Online stands out from the crowd, check out the following information.

Facts About Working At Reinhart FoodService

You can work at Reinhart FoodService when you are eighteen years old. The company typically operates around the clock every day of the year to meet the needs of customers. Employees need to work a variety of shift patterns to ensure continuous service.

Reinhart FoodService Job Opportunities

Reinhart FoodService vacancies can be found in various departments of the company. There are plenty of opportunities for entry-level candidates to master new skills and develop their careers. People with specialist skills and experience are also needed to lead different parts of the company. Here are some of the most common jobs at Reinhart FoodService and the duties that go with them.

Customer Service Representative

This role takes place in a Reinhart FoodService call center and mainly involves talking on the phone. Customer service representatives receive calls from customers as well as orders and pick-up and return requests. It is necessary to be able to operate the company’s computer system to make around $40,000 per year.

Order Selector

These employees work in a Reinhart FoodService warehouse and choose the products that go on pallets. They oversee all orders and make sure that the orders are correct and securely loaded. They work with delivery drivers to make sure that orders reach their intended destination. Order selectors can expect to make around $20,000 per year.

Sales Consultant

These sales professionals handle client profiles to maximize sales and profits. You need to establish a rapport and lasting relationship with individual clients. Sales consultants need to be very organized and have excellent customer service skills.

Sales consultants also cold call potential clients to make sales. The job usually takes place in a call center, although some sales consultants work remotely. The average annual salary is $45,000 per year in addition to performance-related bonuses.

Buyer

The main aim of the game here is to find ways to maximize company profits. Buyers analyze food distribution market trends and suggest new products to sell. They also monitor inventory and maintain relationships with important clients.

Buyers need to have a good understanding of food products and market trends. While a high school diploma takes care of the education requirements, some work experience is also needed. Buyers who work for Reinhart FoodService full-time usually make around $60,000 per year.

Warehouse Receiver

Employees of all levels are needed to work in the Reinhart FoodService busy warehouses. This is an entry-level job that comes with training and advancement opportunities. The starting salary is $14 per hour, which rises to $17 per hour with experience.

Warehouse receivers need to carefully check products when they are delivered to the warehouse. They need to be physically fit and able to lift up to eight pounds. Employees who learn to operate special equipment are entitled to extra pay and benefits.

CDL Class A Driver

This job is perfect for anyone who likes working independently and spending time on the open road. CDL Class A drivers pick up orders from the warehouse and drive them safely to their destination. It is important to follow delivery schedules, keep the vehicle maintained, and pass regular safety checks.

CDL Class A drivers need to hold a clean driver’s license and a professional driving license. It is necessary to be at least 21 years old and be reasonably physically fit to load and unload goods. People who make the grade can make at least $60,000 per year in this role.

Additional Benefits of Working at Reinhart FoodService

Reinhart FoodService employees receive discounts on certain products, free uniforms, and paid training. Eligible full-time professionals receive extra benefits like paid time off and life insurance. Other benefits include vision, dental, and disability insurance and entry into the company pension plan.

How To Apply?

When you visit the careers section of the Reinhart FoodService website, you will see a long list of vacancies. You can narrow the search by selecting a job category or location from the dropdown menu. Click on a job title to read the full job description, including the requirements and overview.

When you have found the perfect role, click on the ‘Apply now’ button. You will then be prompted to sign in, which can be done through a Facebook or LinkedIn account. If you sign in with LinkedIn, your information will be added to the Reinhart FoodService application form.

Make sure that all the information is correct before uploading your resume and clicking to submit your application.

Reinhart FoodService talent community…

It is also possible to save your information to the Reinhart FoodService talent community. You will then receive an email alert when a suitable position is posted. This helps you to get ahead of the competition and increases your chances of getting hired.

Final Thoughts

The interview process at Reinhart FoodService usually starts with a phone screening. People who pass the phone screening will then be asked to attend an in-person interview. If you are not free to talk when you get the call, it is best to arrange a more convenient time.

The in-person interview is usually fairly short and informal, depending on the type of position you are applying for. Hiring managers need to make sure that candidates are committed to excellence and have the necessary skills. Review the job description and requirements before the interview and highlight the necessary skills.
