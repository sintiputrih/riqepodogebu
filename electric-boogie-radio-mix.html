Overall Meaning

The song "Electric Boogie" by Marcia Griffiths is a popular dance anthem that celebrates the power and energy of electric music. The lyrics describe the feeling of being swept up in the rhythm and movement of the electric slide, a popular line dance from the 1970s that was often performed to the song. The repeated refrain of "it's electric" emphasizes the intangible and irresistible quality of electric music, which can only be felt by fully immersing oneself in its energy.


As the song progresses, the lyrics turn to focus on the experience of going to a party and sharing in the joy and excitement of dancing with others. The singer invites the listener to come along on a "party ride" and learn the electric slide, highlighting the communal aspect of dance and the way that music can bring people together. The final repetition of "ola ola eh, ola eh" adds a playful and celebratory touch, underscoring the carefree and joyful vibe of the song.


Overall, "Electric Boogie" is a catchy and uplifting anthem that celebrates the power of music and dance to fill us with energy and joy.
