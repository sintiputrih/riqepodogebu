What gauge jumper cables do I need?

A basic set of jumper cables has a gauge rating of six. The lower the gauge rating, the thicker the cables are. The thicker the wires are, the more effective they’ll be. How can I get my vehicle to start when the battery is dead?

Jump-starting a dead battery is the most popular method of dealing with the situation. To jump-start an automobile, all you need is a pair of jumper wires and another car (preferably owned by a kind stranger) with a functioning battery. Never attempt to jump start a vehicle if the battery is damaged and clearly leaking acid, since this might result in serious injury or death.
