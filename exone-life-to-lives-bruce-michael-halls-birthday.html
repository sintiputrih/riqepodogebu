Former “One Life To Live” actor Bruce Michael Hall (ex-Joey Buchanan, 2003 – 2004) and his twin brother Seth, who took over the role of “Passions'” Reese Durkee for his brother back in 2003, will be celebrating their 35th birthday and want you to join them.

ABC Soaps In Depth is reporting that on Saturday September 10 the guys, along with their band Borderline Country, plan to put on a show in their hometown of Saint Joseph, Missouri at the Buffalo Bar on Highway 59. If you’d like to attend and need more information please call 1-816-390-9797.
