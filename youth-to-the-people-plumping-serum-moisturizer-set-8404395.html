Shoppers Call This 25%-Off Serum-Moisturizer Duo a Thirst Quencher

As someone with dry skin, I know the importance of moisture-rich skincare products in my routine. Hydration makes or breaks the way my complexion looks and feels. And with winter almost here, our skin will need all the hydration it can get, which is why we love Youth to the People’s Youth Stacks: Plump It Up. This two-step kit is specifically formulated for dry, dehydrated skin. And the best part? The brand is offering 25 percent off sitewide ahead of Black Friday, so you can score this set for $21 right now. 

The Youth to the People’s Youth Stacks: Plump It Up duo includes travel-sized versions of the brand’s Triple Peptide + Cactus Oasis Serum and Adaptogen Deep Moisture Cream. The serum is made with three kinds of peptides to firm the areas with loss of elasticity, four types of hyaluronic acid to intensely hydrate, and cactus stem to lock in moisture, per the brand. 

When it comes to a daily moisturizer, the featured Adaptogen Deep Moisture Cream is a solid pick. It’s infused with a powerful trio of squalane, jojoba, and shea butter to boost your skin’s hydration for longer periods of time without clogging pores. Along with this, this sensitive skin-friendly lotion calms visible irritation and redness. 

Youth Stacks: Plump It Up set will feel like a treat for your skin if you’re dealing with dryness, and shoppers agree. Reviewers say it feels so refreshing to apply every day. They rave that it’s a “thirst-quencher” and it  “feels so soft and buttery on skin.” A fan who lives in a “dry Arizona climate” similarly shared that it helps “retain hydration to keep [their] skin soft.” Lastly, a fan who’s prone to breakouts said it’s a “heaven sent” because it’s “beautifully moisturizing without making [their] skin oily.” 

Try the Youth to the People’s Youth Stacks: Plump It Up set that’s just $21 until November 28. What’s not to love about dewy glow at an affordable price?
