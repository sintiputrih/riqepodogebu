90 Day Fiance News - Us Weekly

Network:TLC

90 Day Fiancé puts the pressure on long-distance couples, giving American citizens 90 days to marry their foreign fiancés before their K-1 visas expire. If the couples decide not to exchange vows, the international visitors have to immediately return to their native countries.

90 Day Fiancé premiered on TLC in January 2014 and has since aired nine seasons. The series has spawned more than 20 spinoffs including 90 Day Fiancé: Happily Ever After, which follows the newly married couples after their weddings, 90 Day Fiancé: Before the 90 Days, which documents couples who met online but haven’t started the K-1 visa process, and 90 Day Fiancé: The Other Way, in which the American partner moves to their partner’s home country instead of vice versa.
