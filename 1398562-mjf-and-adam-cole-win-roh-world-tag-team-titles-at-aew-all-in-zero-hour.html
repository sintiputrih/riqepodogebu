MJF And Adam Cole Win ROH World Tag Team Titles At AEW All In: Zero Hour

MJF and Adam Cole won the ROH World Tag Team Championship at AEW All In.

At the Zero Hour pre-show ahead of AEW All In, AEW World Champion MJF and Adam Cole challenged Aussie Open for the ROH World Tag Team Championship. Tensions were high heading into the match, as MJF will defend the AEW World Championship against Adam Cole in the main event of the pay-per-view.

However, MJF and Adam Cole operated like a well-oiled machine, and they had no trouble coexisting throughout the title match. MJF finally hit the Kangaroo Kick, and the duo hit their renowned Double Clothesline for the surprising win.

MJF and Cole celebrated after the bell, but the AEW World Champion also raised his title over his head in a stare-down with Cole.

Aussie Open previously won the titles at ROH Death Before Dishonor 2023.
