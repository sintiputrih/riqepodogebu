Meet Million Dollar Hustle's Bianca Shadai! Bio, Age, Net Worth, Married

Bianca Shadai was one of Stormy Wellington’s clients. After being laid off from a 7-year career at Wall Street and being forced to move into a one-bedroom apartment with her mom, she approached Stormy to get her life back in order. And now that she does, she is also a part of the “Circle of Bosses” alongside Stormy on Million Dollar Hustle.

This Bianca Shadai Bio explores her life. 

Bianca Shadai On Million Dollar Hustle

Lifetime’s Million Dollar Hustle premiered on Mar 17, 2022, and it saw Stormy Wellington aiding the female leaders — personally and financially — succeed in their hustles. Bianca Shadai was one of those “female leaders” who faced off the other participants, proving their worth that they deserved a place in Stormy’s inner elite circle.

Joining Bianca in this inaugural were her co-stars — Dianna Williams, Nathalie Nicole Smith, Ana Cantera, Bawselady, Tammy Price, and Stormy’s very own daughter Maniya Essence.

Bianca first met Stormy as a client who wasn’t feeling the best about her self-image. At the time, Bianca purchased NutraBurst®liquid multivitamin, NRG dietary supplement, and Iaso® Instant detox Tea, and the next thing she knew, she was teaming up socially to do a 30-day weight loss challenge with a cash reward.

And that was the moment when the products went from her “head to heart.” Meeting the girls she never met before, being responsible for changing their lives, she felt empowered.

Eventually, Bianca then met Nathalie Nicole Smith, her sponsor, at one of Stormy’s events. The two immediately connected, admittedly due to similar struggles and since then Bianca’s business has only bloomed.

Having been through so much, it was only natural for Stormy to acknowledge her will to succeed. Thus, she was invited to the “Circle of Bosses” and given a shot a life to be a part of the top entrepreneurs’ circle in the US.

For those wondering how much of the show is real? Well, as for as well know the show’s totally legit. If you feel like Stormy and her fellow Circle of Bosses are a little much at times, it’s only because they share the same confidence which got them where they are today.

Bianca Shadai Net Worth

Bianca Shadai’s reported net worth was around $1 million as of 2022. She was working as the National Director of Total Life Changes for over 2 years then. Her short-term goal then was to start reaching more customers on a global scale, while her long-term goal included her desire to create a strong team of Life Changers who will help them reach more customers.

For those who don’t know, Total Life Changes is a direct selling company offering diverse health and wellness products, founded by Jack Fallon.

Prior to joining TLC, Bianca has a 7-year career at Wall Street. She worked as a full-time “Operations Analyst” with a Fortune 500 company for 3 years and as a “Securities Processing Representative II” at Citi for 4 years.

Also, Bianca offered her clothing collection at “shopbdai.com” all priced below $100.

As for her education, Bianca attended Union County College for her associate of arts degree from 2015 to 2018. She then joined Rutgers University for her bachelor of arts degree and graduated with a 3.8 GPA in 2020.

Is Bianca Shadai Married?

No, Bianca Shadai wasn’t married. But she does have a son named Mark whose father was in jail.

According to Bianca, life was tough after her baby-daddy went to prison and she lost her job. She recalled that there was even a time when she was so ashamed to pull out her WFNJ Families First card to pay for groceries.

But things are different now. So different that she now plans on leaving “generational wealth” for her son.

Her son, Mark turned 11 in October 2021. He is often featured over his IG and TikTok.
